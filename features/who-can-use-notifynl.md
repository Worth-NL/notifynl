# Who can use NotifyNL

**NotifyNL is available for teams from:**

* central government departments
* municipal authorities
* the armed forces
* healthcare institutions
* general practitioner practices
* pharmacies
* emergency services
* state-funded schools

If you work for one of these organisations but receive an error when creating an account, [please contact support](https://admin.notifynl.nl/support).

NotifyNL is not intended for personal use.

**NotifyNL is not available for:**

* charities
* hospices
* housing corporations
* private individuals
* universities

#### Suppliers

Do you work for an organisation in the public sector? Then you can use NotifyNL.

Someone from the public organisation you work for must first create an account and add the service. After that, they can invite you to collaborate as a team member.

#### Private individuals

NotifyNL is only intended for people who work for the government or other public organisations.

Find government services and information on NotifyNL.
