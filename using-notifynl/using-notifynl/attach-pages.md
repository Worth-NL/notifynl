# Attach pages

Upload a PDF and we’ll print it as part of your letter.

You can attach pages with complex layouts. For example, forms, tables or Direct Debit mandates.

To add an attachment to your letter:

1. Go to the Templates page.
2. Add a new letter template or choose an existing template
3. Select Attach pages and choose a file.

Your PDF must meet our attachment specification.

### Attachment specification

Page size and layout: A4 portrait (210 × 297 mm)

Maximum file size: 2 MB

Your letter must be 10 pages or less (5 double-sided sheets of paper).

The content of your letter must appear inside the printable area:

* Left margin 15mm
* Right margin 15mm
* Top margin 5mm
* Bottom margin 5mm
